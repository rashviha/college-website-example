🎓 Who We Are

Founded in the heart of Coimbatore, KG College of Arts and Science stands as a beacon of academic excellence and holistic education. Established in 2005 under the prestigious KG Group, our institution has grown into one of Tamil Nadu’s most sought-after colleges for young thinkers, creators, and innovators.

🌱 Our Vision

To foster an inclusive and innovative academic ecosystem that inspires every student to achieve greatness — not just in academics, but in life.

🎯 Our Mission

  • Deliver high-quality education rooted in values and relevance.
  • Encourage research, innovation, and real-world application.
  • Empower students with skills for tomorrow’s careers.
  • Build a community that celebrates diversity and collaboration.

🏆 Accreditations & Achievements

KG College is NAAC accredited and affiliated with Bharathiar University. With consistent university ranks, international collaborations, and an industry-driven curriculum, our students are thriving in both academia and the professional world.

👩‍🏫 Courses Offered

From traditional degrees to cutting-edge tech programs, we offer:

  • B.Com, BBA, BCA
  • B.Sc (Computer Science, Psychology, Visual Communication)
  • M.Sc, M.Com, MBA (Industry-focused)

🌐 Collaborations & Industry Tie-ups

We partner with leading companies like Infosys, TCS, Wipro, and academic leaders such as Coursera and NPTEL to ensure students are industry-ready and globally competent.

🎭 Campus Life

Life at KG is more than just lectures. From cultural fests and innovation days to sports meets and TEDx events — there’s always something happening. The campus is a blend of modern infrastructure and youthful energy.
